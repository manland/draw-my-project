Features

With 1 grunt task transform your code into graph !

If I modify this file, how files will be impacted ?

Just hovering node to show files (green links) which may be impacted !

Oh yes, a legend to understand more easily.

Click on `Legend` button to show/hide legend !

Oh yes, a filter to easily find node.

Click on `Filter` button to show/hide... Filter ! Amazing not ?

Last but not least : Advices !

Click on `Advices` button to show/hide... Ok you know... ! Available advices.

Why not other kinds of graph ?

Click on `Files view` button to change graph type.

Size chart.

Size chart by type.

Oh yes all charts can select node, of course !

And more...

Works with :

  • AngularJs
  • RequireJs
  • NodeJs (in development)

Parametrable :

  • Your project's logo
  • Your project's name
  • Your advices
